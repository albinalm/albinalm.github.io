“Give a man a program, frustrate him for a day. Teach a man to program, frustrate him for a lifetime.” ― Muhammad Waseem
I am a 22 year old programmer from Alingsås, Sweden. Today I am studying .NET-Development at Teknikhögskolan - Gothenburg
My programming interest ignited when I was around twelve years old and tried to create my own music player. Young and naive I believed that there would be some drag and drop application for me to fulfill my desire.

Unprepared I stumbled across Visual Basic and opened my first project. Not even knowing it was programming I followed YouTube-tutorials and Stackoverflow-guides to patch together my first application. Stubborn as I am, I finally managed to finish my very own music player. The feeling of accomplishement is what ignited my flame, but also what fuels it even today.
Driven by the desire to help others create their software ideas(just like I once wanted), drove me into creating CodeIndent.
